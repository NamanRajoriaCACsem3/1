We often hear the phrase above, when the rapid changes in our daily weather patterns – wildfires, hurricanes, heatwaves, floods, storms, droughts, and so on – are being discussed. These weather events aren’t just increasing in frequency, they are becoming more and more extreme.


‘The Snowball has been set in motion’

Greta Thunberg's words serve as a stark reminder that the impacts of climate change are not distant threats—they're unfolding before our eyes. The inertia of our actions has set a snowball effect in motion, accelerating the pace of global warming and intensifying its consequences.
